Strategy Comparison
Baseline
S1: Status Quo

Every port operates independently. No coordinated repositioning between ports — if a port runs out of containers, it must lease at premium rates. Reflects the current fragmented state.

Recommended
S2: Regional Thresholds

Ports share containers but protect local buffers. A port only repositions surplus containers after its inventory exceeds a threshold (barrier coefficient × capacity). Realistic & practical.

Optimal
S3: National Network

Full national coordination — the optimizer freely moves containers across all ports to minimize total system cost. Represents the theoretical best-case requiring unified digital infrastructure.
